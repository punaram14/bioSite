Basanta's Bio

Basanta Adhikari was born and raised in Kathmandu, Nepal. From a young age, he was fascinated by technology, often exploring how gadgets worked. This curiosity led him to pursue Computer Science, where he excelled in programming and data analysis. Throughout his academic journey, Basanta actively participated in tech events and hackathons, which fueled his passion for data-driven decision-making.

Currently, Basanta works as a Data Analyst at an IT firm. His role involves extracting insights from complex data sets to support strategic business decisions. Despite the demanding nature of his job, he remains an avid cyclist, enjoying the serenity of Kathmandu's hills and valleys on weekends.

Basanta's vision is to establish a data analytics company focused on marketing and branding. Inspired by technopreneurs like Elon Musk, he is constantly learning and expanding his network. His recent read, "The Psychology of Money," has furthered his interest in self-development and finance. Basanta is not just a tech enthusiast; he is a knowledge seeker dedicated to using data to drive growth and innovation.
