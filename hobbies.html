Basanta's Hobby - Cycling

Basanta finds joy and peace in cycling. He often ventures into the hills and valleys around Kathmandu, exploring new routes and enjoying the beauty of nature. Cycling not only provides him with a sense of freedom but also serves as a way to stay fit and unwind from his work routine. Here are some glimpses of his cycling adventures:

Basanta loves to start his day with a refreshing ride through the hills. The cool morning breeze and the breathtaking sunrise views are his motivation to wake up early and embrace the day with energy.

Always up for an adventure, Basanta enjoys discovering new trails and paths. He takes his bike off the beaten track to find hidden gems in nature, which gives him a sense of excitement and achievement.

Cycling through the valleys of Kathmandu, Basanta enjoys the diverse landscapes and the challenge of navigating different terrains. These journeys give him a chance to reflect, relax, and connect with nature.
